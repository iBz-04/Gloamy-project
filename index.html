Discover Istanbul

The city where continents meet, history breathes, and beauty endures.

History

Istanbul, formerly Byzantium and Constantinople, is a city that has been the capital of three empires: Roman, Byzantine, and Ottoman. Its unique location on both Europe and Asia has made it a crossroads of civilizations for over 2,500 years.

Top Sights

  • Hagia Sophia – A marvel of architecture, once a church, mosque, and now a museum.
  • Blue Mosque – Famous for its stunning blue tiles and six minarets.
  • Bosphorus – The strait dividing Europe and Asia, best seen by ferry.
  • Grand Bazaar – One of the world’s oldest and largest covered markets.
  • Galata Tower – Offers panoramic views of the city skyline.
